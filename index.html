2️⃣ Simple Blackjack Game

Game Rules

  1. Game starts with two random cards in hand
  2. Maximum Point limit is 21
  3. Sum of card value is less than 21 you can continue or call out.
  4. Sum of card value is 21 you can call blackjack or continue.
  5. Sum of card value is greater than 21 you can bluff or call out.
  6. Start with 50 points.
  7. Each bet is made with 10 points.
  8. Point reaches 0 will automaticaly disqualify player.
